Who Am I?

I'm Ben Francis Charles Brian Porter, a medical student currently taking a year out to study an intercalated degree in Anatomy, with wider interests in software development, and business & project management. Not only do I hold interests in these areas, but have acted upon them by actively pursuing them over the last four or so years, working my way up in a MedEd/MedTech start-up called Medify. This has been a huge learning experience and has provided me with great insight into the workings of such a company; it has allowed me to gain extensive experience in both working in, and managing, large content-creation teams and their respective workflows. This website acts as somewhat of an "online resume/CV" for me to detail all of my accomplishments so far, and to outline the projects that I have worked on so far during my time in this industry.

As this is the home page, I shall not digress much further and shall allow you to explore the site. I have separated it into various sections that should make it easier to find some of the projects I have worked on both on a corporate scale and some of my personal development projects which have allowed me to gain extensive coding experience in the downtime between other work.
